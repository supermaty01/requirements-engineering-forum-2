Welcome to Expectation Madness!

In this game you are requirements analysts capturing the expectations of the project stakeholder. A meeting will be held where the you will compete against each other to get the job.

Rules:

  1. The stakeholder meeting will begin when all analysts introduce themselves. After this, one analyst will present one creativity trigger card at a time, this will be done in the order in which they were introduced
  2. When an analyst presents a creativity trigger card to the stakeholder, the stakeholder will immediately say the collection of concepts corresponding to that innovation topic.
  3. After the stakeholder says the collection of concepts, the analyst has only thirty (30) seconds to form a structured expectation based on these concepts. If the analyst does not form the expectation, it is up to the next analyst to decide what creativity trigger card to present to the stakeholder.
  4. The analyst must use all the concepts to form the structured expectation
  5. After the analyst forms the expectation, the stakeholder will tell you whether or not he/she is satisfied with it. If the stakeholder is satisfied, the expectation will be considered to have been captured correctly and will be assigned to the analyst. After this, another analyst will decide what creativity trigger card to present to the analyst
  6. When an expectation is captured, the collection of concepts associated with this expectation will no longer be said by the stakeholder.
  7. If the analyst cannot capture the expectation correctly, its concept collection associated may be said again by the stakeholder.
  8. Each creativity trigger will foster a maximum of 3 concept collections in the stakeholder, once the associated expectations have been captured, the creativity trigger card cannot be chosen again.

Instructions:

  1. Click the "Select analyst" button to begin the game.
  2. The analyst marked with orange color in the right side of the screen has the turn.
  3. The analyst with the turn must click on a light bulb to display a creativity trigger.
  4. Once the analyst have finished reading the creativity trigger, must click the "Ready!" button.
  5. The analyst has 30 seconds to reorder the words and write them down on the "Expectation" field.
  6. Finish the game by clicking on the finish line on the top left of the screen

Winner Selection Criteria:

The analyst with the most expectations is the winner.
